AutoHub Multi-Brand Dealership - Site Plan

Website Project planning document

Site Name

AutoHub Multi-Brand Dealership

This name was chosen because it clearly reflects a professional car dealership showcasing multiple automobile brands under one unified platform.

Optional domain: autohub-dealer.com

Site Purpose

The website will provide a modern car showroom experience, highlighting multiple car brands, vehicle inventories, specifications, dealership services, and a contact/inquiry system. It aims to help customers compare options, schedule visits, and explore brand details seamlessly.

Scenarios

  • Which available vehicles fall within my preferred budget and brand choice?
  • How can I contact the dealership or schedule a test drive?
